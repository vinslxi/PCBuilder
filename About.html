About Me

I am Elijah Vinson, and I strongly admire the technology that runs our world today. I developed this site to inform other people about how computers function internally, the functions of their internal parts and how engineering principles allow them to perform effectively. It is important to know the functionality of every component; CPU, GPU, memory, storage, and power supply just to mention a few, to anyone who wishes to build or upgrade his/her own computer. It is important to me to make this information clear, accurate and easy to understand even by the people who are new to the computer hardware field. While learning about system design and performance optimization, I have become deeply interested in the interaction between hardware and software to produce balanced, high-performance systems. I can say that assembling a computer is not only an effective technique but also a means of training problem-solving skills and gaining a better understanding of the entire technology. Here, you can read in-depth descriptions, build tutorials, and tips that are easily understood to make better choices when choosing parts or debugging problems. Whether it is your first PC or you are seeking the know-how of how your existing system functions, this site intends to give you the know-how and the confidence to start building your own computer. I would like to see more people discover, create and innovate in the ever-changing computing world to make computer technology more accessible.
