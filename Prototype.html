This prototype demonstrates a simple “PC Builder Helper” that suggests parts based on the user’s budget and primary use (gaming, content creation, or everyday).

Goal

Help beginners choose compatible parts quickly, avoid common mistakes, and see an estimated price before buying.

How It Works

  1. User selects a budget range and use case.
  2. The prototype maps to a pre-vetted parts list (CPU, GPU, RAM, storage, PSU, case).
  3. It shows an estimated total and links to beginner setup guides.

Note: This is a front-end demo; no purchases are made.
